Frequently Asked Questions

1. How many lessons do I need to pass the test?

Everyone learns at a different pace, but I can assess your skills and estimate how many lessons you may require.

2. Can I use my own car?

Yes, if it meets safety standards and is fully insured. Otherwise, you can use one of my dual-control vehicles.

3. What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

I understand life happens. Just let me know 24 hours in advance, and I’ll find a new time.

4. Do you offer weekend or evening lessons?

Absolutely. I offer flexible schedules to suit busy lifestyles.
